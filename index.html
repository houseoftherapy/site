Got Questions?

Is Cupping painful?


Hijama, also known as cupping therapy, involves placing cups on the skin to create suction. Many individuals report feeling a tight sensation or pulling during the cupping process, but it is generally considered tolerable. While some people find it uncomfortable, others may find it relaxing. If you experience pain at any point, please let our practitioner know immediately!
